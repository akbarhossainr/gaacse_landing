Mission

Alumni Association of CSE, GUB is to provide an incentive for members to direct highly qualified and motivated potential candidates to the CSE department; to aid the Department in its future development both in an advisory and financial capacity; to sponsor social and professional activities of the Alumni Association and also to involve in various activities to serve the society along with the current students of the Department.

Vision

The Alumni Association of CSE, GUB shall promote the interests, welfare and educational aims of GUB and its alumni, establish and maintain a mutually beneficial relationship between CSE Department and its alumni, and encourage lifelong engagement of CSE, GUB alumni with their fellow alumni and the University community.

Our Goals

  • Organizing alumni programs mutually beneficial to alumni and current students of the Department.
  • Keeping up-to-date about the activities of CSE, GUB
  • Giving feedback to the Department in a way that impacting the future academic and pro-career activities.
  • Providing financial and technical supports to the poor students of CSE and to develop infrastructure facilities.
